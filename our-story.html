Who We Are?

we are a group of 2 members which helps poor people by feeding them. We have been doing this work for 12 years. There are other people involved in this work as well.Our team goes to different places and deliver food to the poor people. We have a small cold storage where we bring leftover food from venues which are located near us.

What We Are Doing?

We take leftover food from weddings, birthday parties & other events. Firstly, we check the freshness & quality of food then we give it to poor or needy. We request everyone not to throw the leftover food, rather than provide it to needy. You can also donate money for feeding the poor.

what we do with rotten throat food....

After distribution of food some food which is rotten throat & can not be used for eating . we compost that food & make manure from that food.

some steps we follow to compost food....

* Add a layer of branches at the bottom.
*Add old compost\soil.
*Use a hot water bottle to kick start compost.
*Use a compost duvet.
*Turn compost.
*Create free air space in compost.
*Adding nitrogen rich material.
*Getting the moisture ratio right.
*Compost activators.
*Add easily digestible materials.
*Reduce material size.
*Increase the volume of pile.
*Use the berkeley method.
*Insulated bins.
*Add biochar to compost.
*Add worms to finish off this process.
